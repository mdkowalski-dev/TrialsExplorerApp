Privacy Policy

English

CT2 does not require an account and does not collect or sell personal data.

  • Data you provide: none.
  • Data stored on your device: favorites and app settings are stored locally (on-device).
  • Network requests: the app retrieves public data from ClinicalTrials.gov and publication metadata from PubMed. Your device may send standard technical information (e.g., IP address) to these services as part of normal internet communication.
  • Tracking: no tracking across apps and websites.
  • Analytics / ads: none.

Medical disclaimer: CT2 is for informational and research purposes only and does not provide medical advice.

If you have questions about privacy, email: michal.kowalski@fastmail.com
